Contribute to SwimOS

The SwimOS community welcomes contributions from anyone with a passion for streaming data applications.

There are many opportunities to contribute to SwimOS:

  • Contribute code. Check out the open issue list and see if there's something you'd like to work on. You can also suggest new features that you'd be interested in working on.
  • Contribute example projects & tutorials. We're always on the lookout for folks who are using SwimOS in production. If you have a working demo, please consider adding it to the demos page or writing a tutorial about it.
  • Contribute to the docs. We're in the process of re-vamping our documentation site. If you've noticed a gap in content, we'd love for you to open an issue or contribute content.

You can also engage with the community on Slack. You can get an invite to our Slack workspace here.

Not sure where to start?

If you'd like to contribute but aren't sure where to start, feel free to reach out to us on Slack. We can walk you through our various repositories and libraries and help you find something to work on.
